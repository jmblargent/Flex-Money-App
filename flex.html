    The Office of the Registrar at LeTourneau University exists to serve the academic needs of all LeTourneau University students. Our office handles graduation, keeps record of grades, supplies transcripts, works with the Department of Veteran Affairs and provides multiple other services for the students of LeTourneau University.   For more information, visit our Registrar Website.
