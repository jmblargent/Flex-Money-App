    Traditional Students

    The Student Life Office is open from 8:00 am - 5:00 pm Monday-Friday. The office is located on the second floor of the Allen Family Student Center.  

    Student Photo/ID: Contact Wanda Davis at (903) 233-4400.

    Emergency Contact Info: Contact Wanda Davis at (903) 233-4400.
     

    The Health Services Department is open from 8:00 am - 12:00 pm and 1:00 pm - 3:00 pm Monday-Friday. Nurse Jerrie Reynolds' office is located within the Student Life Office in the Allen Family Student Center. 

    Immunization Records: Contact Wanda Davis at (903) 233-4400.
     
     
    University Police Department is located on Harris Street behind the Business Office.

    Police Chief:  Michael Schultz is available from 8:00 am - 5:00 pm Monday - Friday at (903) 233-4441.

    24 Hour Calls For Service: Dial extension 4444 or call (903) 233-4444.
